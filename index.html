About Us

Founded in 2003, BestLum Lighting Technologies Company is a professional manufacturer located in Shangyu, Shaoxing City, Zhejiang Province, China. Our production workshop covers over 12,000 square meters, enabling us to support high-volume manufacturing with consistent quality control.

We specialize in the development and production of Emergency Lighting Fixtures, serving a wide range of commercial and industrial applications. As a trusted OEM/ODM supplier, we provide custom lighting solutions tailored to the specific needs of our global clients across Asia, Europe, and the Americas.

BestLum products are certified to meet international quality and safety standards, including CB, CE, EMC, and UKCA approvals. We are ISO-certified, and our production processes are designed to ensure reliability, efficiency, and competitive pricing.
